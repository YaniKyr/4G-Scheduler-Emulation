Practical Allocation of RBs:

    In LTE, the minimum allocation is 1 RB per TTI, but in reality, many more RBs can be allocated to a single UE, especially when the user requires high data rates or when the network load is low.

    For example, depending on the bandwidth of the LTE carrier (e.g., 10 MHz, 20 MHz), the total number of available RBs per TTI can range from 50 to 100 RBs, and a single user can be allocated several tens of RBs.
